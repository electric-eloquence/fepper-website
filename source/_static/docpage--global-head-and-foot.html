Global Head and Foot

The following files will define the HTML head and foot for all patterns:

  • source/_meta/_00-head.mustache
  • source/_meta/_01-foot.mustache

You are free to edit these files, but...

Do not remove either of these lines:

  • {{{ patternlabHead }}}
  • {{{ patternlabFoot }}}

Fepper needs these to work.

Otherwise, to see edits to the global head or foot, restart or manually build Fepper.
